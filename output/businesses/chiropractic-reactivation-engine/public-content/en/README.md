# Public Content — EN — Chiropractic Patient Reactivation Engine

## Core build-in-public angle

“Before a chiropractic clinic spends another dollar on new-patient ads, it should know how many inactive patients could be safely and respectfully invited back.”

## Audience

- Chiropractic clinic owners.
- Office managers and patient coordinators.
- Healthcare marketing agencies serving local clinics.

## Content pillars

1. **Existing-patient revenue** — reactivation as a measurable alternative to always buying new leads.
2. **Respectful recall** — messages that sound human and avoid pressure, diagnosis, or medical claims.
3. **Staff approval** — AI drafts, clinic staff approves.
4. **Segmented outreach** — different messages for 90-day, 6-month, and 12-month inactive patients.
5. **Compliance-first operations** — opt-outs, audit logs, minimal patient data, and escalation rules.

## Sample post ideas

- “A reactivation campaign is not ‘blast the patient list.’ It is segmentation, staff approval, opt-out handling, and appointment tracking.”
- “The first ROI metric: how many inactive patients booked and attended after respectful recall?”
- “AI should draft chiropractic reactivation messages, not give care advice.”

## Landing CTA

Run a mock inactive-patient audit: list size, inactivity windows, historical visit value, and current recall process. Use aggregate numbers only until a compliant pilot is ready.
