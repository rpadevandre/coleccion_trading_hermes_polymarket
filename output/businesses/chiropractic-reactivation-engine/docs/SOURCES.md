## Internal usage notes

- Keep all repo examples synthetic; do not include patient names, diagnoses, phone numbers, or appointment histories.
- Validate with clinics using aggregate numbers first: inactive list size, reachable percentage, booked appointments, attended visits, and collected revenue.
- Treat compliance as a sales requirement: staff approval, opt-out handling, minimal PHI, and audit logs are part of the product, not afterthoughts.
