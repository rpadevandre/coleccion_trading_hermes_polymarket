# Frontend Routes

- `/chiropractic-reactivation-engine/landing` — Public page for reactivating inactive patients without spam.
- `/chiropractic-reactivation-engine/recoverycalculator` — Estimate recovered appointments from inactive patient list.
- `/chiropractic-reactivation-engine/campaignpreview` — Show patient segment and human-approved message flow.
- `/chiropractic-reactivation-engine/inactivelistauditform` — Collect list size, visit recency and campaign history.
- `/chiropractic-reactivation-engine/pilotsignup` — Request a limited reactivation pilot.
