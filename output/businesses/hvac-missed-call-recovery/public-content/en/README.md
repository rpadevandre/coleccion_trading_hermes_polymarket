# Public Content — EN — HVAC Missed-Call Recovery

## Core build-in-public angle

“Small HVAC shops do not need another dashboard first. They need proof that unanswered calls are turning into booked jobs instead of voicemail leaks.”

## Audience

- HVAC owner/operators with 3–15 technicians.
- Dispatch managers dealing with peak-season overload.
- Local service marketing agencies looking for a measurable retention/recovery offer.

## Content pillars

1. **Missed-call math** — simple examples showing how a few unanswered emergency calls can exceed the monthly tool cost.
2. **Speed-to-lead operations** — callbacks in 5 minutes vs same-day vs next-day.
3. **Dispatcher relief** — AI summaries that reduce repetitive intake, not replace the dispatcher.
4. **After-hours triage** — routing urgent calls without pretending the AI is a technician.
5. **Pilot transparency** — publish mock dashboards and validation learnings without client data.

## Sample post ideas

- “If an AC outage call hits voicemail at 7:30pm, your competitor may already be booking it.”
- “The first MVP metric for this HVAC tool is not AI accuracy. It is booked jobs recovered from missed calls.”
- “A missed-call system should show three numbers: missed calls, callbacks under 15 minutes, and jobs booked.”

## Landing CTA

Request a free missed-call audit using last month’s call count, missed-call count, average ticket, and booking rate. No real data should be posted publicly; collect only through approved intake later.
