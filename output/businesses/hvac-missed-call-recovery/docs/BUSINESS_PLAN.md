# Business Plan — HVAC Missed-Call Recovery

## Goal

Build a small, monetizable AI workflow product/service for an English-speaking market with clear willingness to pay.

## Target buyer

Owner/operator or operations manager at 3-15 technician HVAC companies in US/Canada/UK/Australia.

## Pain

Urgent HVAC customers call after hours or during service peaks. If the call goes to voicemail, they often call the next contractor.

## Offer

Done-for-you AI missed-call recovery and after-hours triage: call/SMS intake, emergency qualification, routing rules, job summaries and ROI tracking.

## Revenue model hypothesis

- Setup fee for done-for-you configuration.
- Monthly subscription/support.
- Optional paid pilot before full productization.

## Validation gate

Do not overbuild until there is at least one of:

- 2 discovery calls.
- 1 paid pilot/LOI.
- 5+ meaningful replies from target buyers.
- 1 operator willing to test fake/manual MVP flow.
