# Frontend Routes

- `/hvac-missed-call-recovery/landing` — Public page explaining lost revenue from missed HVAC calls and after-hours voicemail.
- `/hvac-missed-call-recovery/roicalculator` — Estimate missed-call revenue leak from average ticket size, missed calls/week and close rate.
- `/hvac-missed-call-recovery/democallflow` — Visual walkthrough of after-hours call capture, triage and dispatch notification.
- `/hvac-missed-call-recovery/freeauditform` — Collect company size, service area, call volume and current after-hours process.
- `/hvac-missed-call-recovery/pilotsignup` — Convert qualified operators into a manual/concierge pilot.
