# HVAC Missed-Call Recovery

**Score:** 75/90

## English-first positioning

Stop losing emergency HVAC jobs to voicemail.

## Spanish adaptation

Deja de perder trabajos urgentes de HVAC por llamadas no contestadas.

## Target buyer

Owner/operator or operations manager at 3-15 technician HVAC companies in US/Canada/UK/Australia.

## Target user

Dispatcher, office admin, on-call technician, owner.

## Problem

Urgent HVAC customers call after hours or during service peaks. If the call goes to voicemail, they often call the next contractor.

## Offer v0

Done-for-you AI missed-call recovery and after-hours triage: call/SMS intake, emergency qualification, routing rules, job summaries and ROI tracking.
