# Validation Plan — MSP Security Reporting Copilot

## Before building deeply

1. Collect public examples of MSP monthly reports and vCISO executive summaries.
2. Identify 20 MSPs with weak/unclear security reporting positioning.
3. Offer a free “monthly security report cleanup” review.
4. Ask for sanitized sample report/ticket export.
5. Generate a before/after mock report using fake data.

## Outreach angle

**EN:**

> Most MSPs do real security work, but the monthly client report often makes that work look like a pile of alerts and ticket counts. I’m building a reporting copilot that turns technical activity into executive-ready client updates.

**ES:**

> Muchos MSPs hacen trabajo real de seguridad, pero el reporte mensual al cliente termina pareciendo una lista de alertas y tickets. Estoy construyendo un copilot que convierte esa actividad técnica en reportes ejecutivos claros.

## Success signal

A target MSP says: “If this saves me report prep time and improves client renewal conversations, I would pay for it.”

## Kill signal

If MSPs do not share sanitized examples, do not care about reporting, or only want direct integrations before any paid pilot, keep this as later-stage opportunity.
