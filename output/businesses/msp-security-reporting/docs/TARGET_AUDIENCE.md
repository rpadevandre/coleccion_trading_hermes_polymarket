# Target Audience — MSP Security Reporting Copilot

## Buyer

Small MSP owners and vCISO/service delivery leads serving SMB clients.

## User

MSP technicians, service delivery managers, vCISO operators, account managers and client-facing report reviewers.

## ICP

- 5-50 employee MSP.
- Serves 20-200 SMB clients.
- Offers security stack, endpoint, backup, ticketing, monitoring or compliance-adjacent services.
- Reports monthly or quarterly to clients but spends too much time editing reports.
- Wants to increase perceived value and reduce churn.

## Why English-speaking market first

US/Canada/UK/Australia MSPs commonly operate on recurring managed-service retainers, have high labor costs, and already pay for SaaS/security tooling. Clear report automation can be positioned as time savings plus retention support.

## Spanish angle

Useful later for Hispanic-owned MSPs or bilingual client reporting, but EN-first is better for pricing and distribution.
