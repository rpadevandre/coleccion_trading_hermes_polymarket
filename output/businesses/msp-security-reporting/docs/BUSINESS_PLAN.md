# Business Plan — MSP Security Reporting Copilot

## Goal

Build a monetizable AI reporting workflow for small MSPs that need to prove security value to SMB clients without spending hours manually translating technical noise into executive summaries.

## Target buyer

Small MSP owners, vCISO operators, service delivery managers and security leads serving SMB clients.

## Pain

MSPs do valuable technical work, but clients often only see confusing alerts, ticket counts or generic PDFs. Poor communication creates churn risk, weak renewal conversations and underpriced security service packages.

## Offer

AI-assisted monthly security reporting copilot that ingests ticket/security exports and creates approval-ready client reports with evidence gaps, risk summaries and next-month priorities.

## Revenue model hypothesis

- Setup fee: $500-$2,000 for report template/import configuration.
- Monthly: $199-$799 depending on client count.
- Premium: white-label client portal and vCISO narrative packs.

## Validation gate

Do not overbuild until there is at least one of:

- 2 MSP discovery calls.
- 1 MSP willing to share sanitized sample reports/exports.
- 1 paid pilot for one client monthly report.
- 5+ meaningful replies from MSP owners/operators.

## Why this can make money

MSPs already charge recurring fees and understand software subscriptions. If the tool saves report preparation time and improves client perceived value, it can justify USD pricing quickly.
