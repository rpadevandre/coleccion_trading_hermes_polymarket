# Brand Kit — MSP Security Reporting Copilot

## Working names

- SecureReport AI
- StackSignal Reports
- MSP ProofDesk
- ClientShield Reports
- vCISO Brief

## Positioning line

**EN:** Turn technical security noise into client-ready monthly reports.

**ES:** Convierte ruido técnico de ciberseguridad en reportes mensuales claros para clientes.

## Tone

Professional, precise, trustworthy, executive-friendly, security-aware, not fearmongering.

## Palette

- Midnight navy: #0F172A
- Trust blue: #2563EB
- Signal cyan: #06B6D4
- Risk amber: #F59E0B
- Clean surface: #F8FAFC
- Text: #111827

## Anti-branding

- No hacker cliché.
- No red-alert panic aesthetic.
- No vague “AI security magic”.
- No claims of replacing security expertise.
