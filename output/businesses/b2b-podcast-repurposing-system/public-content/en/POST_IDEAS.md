# EN Build-in-Public Ideas — B2B Podcast Repurposing System

1. I’m exploring a workflow for B2B SaaS founder, agency owner, consultant, podcast host, content lead..
2. The pain: B2B founders record podcasts or calls but fail to turn them into consistent LinkedIn/X/email content because repurposing is time-consuming.
3. The first MVP is not “AI magic.” It is structured intake, human review and measurable operational improvement.
4. Positioning line: Turn one B2B conversation into a week of sharp content.
