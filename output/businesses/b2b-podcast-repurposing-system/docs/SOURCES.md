## Notes for internal use

The consistent market signal is not "podcast editing" as a commodity. The stronger wedge is: turn underused founder/SME interviews into sales-assist assets, LinkedIn posts, newsletter snippets, SEO briefs, and enablement clips that can be reviewed quickly by a lean marketing team.
