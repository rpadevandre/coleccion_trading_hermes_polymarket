# Validation Plan — B2B Podcast Repurposing System

## Validation thesis

Lean B2B teams already create expensive long-form conversations but fail to convert them into pipeline-supporting assets. They will pay for a reliable workflow if the offer is framed as sales-content leverage, not generic podcast editing.

## Ideal buyer for validation

- Founder-led B2B SaaS company with an active or semi-active podcast/webinar/interview show.
- Agency or consultancy selling high-ticket services and using thought leadership to support sales.
- Marketing lead with 1-5 people and no dedicated content repurposing team.
- English-speaking markets first: US, Canada, UK, Australia.

## Money-focused offers to test

1. **Paid sample pack** — $250-$500 for one episode, delivered as reviewed Markdown pack.
2. **Two-episode pilot** — $750-$1,500, includes intake call, voice profile, 40+ draft assets, review session.
3. **Monthly engine** — $1,500-$4,000/month for 4-8 episodes with asset review workflow.
4. **Sales enablement add-on** — $500/month to produce prospect follow-up snippets and objection assets.

## Success metrics

- 10 qualified conversations in 14 days.
- 3 paid pilot commitments or deposits.
- At least 50% of prospects say they have unused podcast/webinar content.
- At least 2 prospects ask for recurring monthly package without heavy custom dev.
- Delivery economics: under 2 internal hours per episode after workflow stabilizes.

## Outreach experiment

- Build a list of 100 B2B companies with recent podcasts/webinars and visible founder/SME content.
- Send personalized email referencing one episode and offering a sample pack.
- LinkedIn follow-up to founder/marketing lead with one concrete repurposing idea.
- Manual audit loom optional: show 3 assets that could come from their latest episode.

## Interview questions

- How many long-form episodes/webinars did you publish in the last 90 days?
- What happens after an episode goes live?
- Who turns it into posts, emails, clips, or sales material?
- What content formats actually influence pipeline today?
- What would make repurposing not worth it?
- Would you pay for a two-episode pilot this month? If not, why?

## Smoke test landing page

CTA options:
- "Get a paid sample content pack from one episode."
- "Book a 15-minute podcast content audit."
- "Send us an episode; we will return 10 repurposing ideas."

Capture budget signal with:
- episodes per month,
- current content spend,
- deadline,
- whether sales team needs reusable snippets.

## Disqualifiers

- Consumer entertainment podcasts with no monetization path.
- Teams expecting auto-posting with no review.
- Customers requiring direct access to confidential sales calls before trust is established.
- Prospects who only want low-cost clip editing and no written/business assets.
