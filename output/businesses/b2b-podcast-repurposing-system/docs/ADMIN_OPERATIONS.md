# Admin Operations — B2B Podcast Repurposing System

## Daily operating checklist

1. Review failed ingest/transcription/drafting jobs.
2. Check episodes stuck in review for more than 48 hours.
3. Sample at least 5% of generated assets for quote accuracy, claim safety, and voice match.
4. Review customer support requests and impersonation logs.
5. Update pilot tracker: sample delivered, pilot accepted, monthly conversion, churn risk.

## Weekly operating checklist

- Report episode-to-asset yield by workspace.
- Identify workspaces with low approval rates and tune brand rules.
- Review prompt/model changes against QA notes.
- Check storage costs and raw media retention jobs.
- Summarize paid validation outcomes: pilots sold, conversion reasons, objections, expansion requests.

## Human review rules

- Do not publish content directly from the system.
- Do not invent metrics, customer claims, or guest credentials.
- Any asset containing a numerical claim must be tied to the transcript, customer-provided proof, or marked for verification.
- Any quote must include source timestamp in the editor.
- Guest/company spelling must be checked before export.

## Customer onboarding

1. Create workspace.
2. Capture brand voice and examples.
3. Configure forbidden claims, competitor exclusions, CTAs, and retention policy.
4. Ingest one test episode.
5. Review sample pack with customer and update voice profile.
6. Only then process pilot/monthly episode queue.

## Support playbooks

- **Bad transcript**: rerun transcription or request customer transcript; do not generate final assets from low-confidence transcript.
- **Weak drafts**: adjust campaign goal, voice profile, and moment score threshold; regenerate selected asset types only.
- **Claim concern**: mark affected assets as needs_edits and add verification note.
- **Customer wants auto-posting**: defer; current internal business emphasizes review/export, not deployment.

## Metrics to maintain

- Time from episode ingest to review-ready pack.
- Assets generated per episode.
- Approval rate by asset type.
- Average edit cycles per asset.
- Paid pilot conversion rate.
- Monthly expansion/churn reasons.
