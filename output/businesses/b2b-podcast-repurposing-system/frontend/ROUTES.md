# Frontend Routes

- `/b2b-podcast-repurposing-system/landing` — Public page for turning one B2B conversation into a week of content.
- `/b2b-podcast-repurposing-system/beforeafterdemo` — Show transcript fragment transformed into posts/thread/newsletter.
- `/b2b-podcast-repurposing-system/transcriptuploaddemo` — Demo source upload/paste and output selection.
- `/b2b-podcast-repurposing-system/contentcalendarpreview` — Show generated weekly publishing queue.
- `/b2b-podcast-repurposing-system/episodeauditform` — Collect audience, channel, tone and sample transcript.
