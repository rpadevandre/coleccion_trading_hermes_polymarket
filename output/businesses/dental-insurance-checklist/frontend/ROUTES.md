# Frontend Routes

- `/dental-insurance-checklist/landing` — Public page around reducing insurance verification mistakes and rework.
- `/dental-insurance-checklist/checklistdemo` — Show a verification checklist generated from patient/procedure context.
- `/dental-insurance-checklist/reworkcalculator` — Estimate admin time lost to missing insurance information.
- `/dental-insurance-checklist/officeauditform` — Collect office size, procedure mix and verification workflow.
- `/dental-insurance-checklist/pilotsignup` — Request a controlled checklist pilot.
