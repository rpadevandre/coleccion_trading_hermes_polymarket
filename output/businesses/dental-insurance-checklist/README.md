# Dental Insurance Checklist Assistant

**Score:** 62/90

## English-first positioning

Make dental insurance verification less painful before the patient arrives.

## Spanish adaptation

Haz menos dolorosa la verificación de seguro dental antes de que llegue el paciente.

## Target buyer

Dental practice owner, office manager, small DSO operations lead.

## Target user

Front desk, treatment coordinator, insurance coordinator.

## Problem

Dental teams manually verify eligibility/benefits and deal with denied claims, patient confusion and same-day surprises.

## Offer v0

Pre-visit dental benefits checklist workflow: patient intake, verification status, payer checklist, benefit-summary templates and PMS-ready notes.
