# Business Plan — Dental Insurance Checklist Assistant

## Goal

Build a small, monetizable AI workflow product/service for an English-speaking market with clear willingness to pay.

## Target buyer

Dental practice owner, office manager, small DSO operations lead.

## Pain

Dental teams manually verify eligibility/benefits and deal with denied claims, patient confusion and same-day surprises.

## Offer

Pre-visit dental benefits checklist workflow: patient intake, verification status, payer checklist, benefit-summary templates and PMS-ready notes.

## Revenue model hypothesis

- Setup fee for done-for-you configuration.
- Monthly subscription/support.
- Optional paid pilot before full productization.

## Validation gate

Do not overbuild until there is at least one of:

- 2 discovery calls.
- 1 paid pilot/LOI.
- 5+ meaningful replies from target buyers.
- 1 operator willing to test fake/manual MVP flow.
