# Landing Copy — Dental Insurance Checklist Assistant

## Primary Headline

Stop finding insurance surprises while the patient is already in the chair.

## Subheadline

A lightweight checklist workflow for dental offices that turns upcoming appointments into verified benefits, PMS-ready notes and clear patient follow-up tasks before the visit.

## Target Buyer

Dental practice owners, office managers and small DSO operators in English-speaking markets that lose staff time and treatment revenue to manual verification.

## Above-the-Fold CTA

- Primary: `Join the pilot waitlist`
- Secondary: `See the 5-minute workflow`

## Proof-Oriented Problem Bullets

- Insurance details are scattered across patient forms, payer portals, phone calls and memory.
- Treatment coordinators need fast answers on maximums, deductibles, frequency limits and waiting periods.
- Same-day surprises create awkward money conversations and avoidable claim rework.
- Manual verification is hard to scale when front-desk staff are already answering phones.

## MVP Value Proposition

- One queue for visits that need benefits checked.
- Procedure-specific checklist for common high-value appointments.
- Draft note templates for PMS entry and patient communication.
- Exception workflow for inactive coverage, missing subscriber data or coordination-of-benefits issues.
- Simple metrics: checks completed, blockers found, estimated admin time saved.

## Section Copy

### Built for small offices

No enterprise rev-cycle transformation. Start with one office, one checklist and the next week of appointments.

### Human-reviewed by design

The system drafts checklists and notes; office staff approve final insurance language before anything touches the patient record.

### Money-focused validation

Pilot success is measured by fewer same-day insurance surprises, faster pre-visit prep and more high-value visits ready for financial discussion.
