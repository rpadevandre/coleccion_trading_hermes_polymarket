# Outreach — Dental Insurance Checklist Assistant

## ICP

- English-speaking dental practices with 1-8 providers.
- Offices doing crowns, implants, perio, ortho consults or other high-value treatment that needs benefits clarity.
- Buyers: owner-dentist, office manager, treatment coordinator lead, small DSO operations lead.
- Exclude: enterprise DSOs, offices demanding full payer/PMS integration before pilot, practices unwilling to avoid PHI in early demos.

## Money Question

"If this prevented 5 same-day insurance surprises per month and saved your coordinator 3-5 hours weekly, would it be worth $99-$299/month?"

## Cold Email

Subject: quick insurance-verification pilot for {{practice_name}}

Hi {{first_name}},

I am testing a lightweight workflow for dental offices that turns next week's appointments into a benefits checklist, exception queue and PMS-ready note drafts before the patient arrives.

It is not a claims tool and it does not replace your coordinator. The goal is simpler: fewer same-day insurance surprises and less repeated phone/portal work.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to see if your current verification process is painful enough for a small pilot?

Best,
{{sender}}

## LinkedIn DM

Quick question: who owns insurance verification at your office? I am validating a checklist workflow for dental teams to catch benefit gaps before appointments. Looking for blunt feedback from office managers/treatment coordinators, not selling an enterprise system.

## Discovery Questions

1. How many visits per week require insurance/benefits verification?
2. Which procedures create the most financial surprises?
3. How often do you discover inactive coverage or missing subscriber details on appointment day?
4. What does a clean PMS note need to include?
5. What is the dollar impact of one delayed or reworked high-value case?
6. Which payer details are hardest to confirm reliably?
7. Would a human-reviewed AI draft be acceptable if staff approve final wording?
8. What price would be a no-brainer if it saved 3-5 staff hours weekly?

## Validation Channels

- Local dental office manager Facebook groups, using value-first questions only.
- Dental practice management consultants.
- Small DSO ops leaders on LinkedIn.
- Existing dental billing/RCM communities.
- Cold outreach to offices with visible high-value services pages.

## No-Go Signals

- Buyer only wants fully automated eligibility integrations in MVP.
- Offices cannot run any pilot without real PHI before compliance work.
- Staff do not perceive verification as a money/time pain.
- Existing PMS workflow already solves the checklist problem cleanly.
