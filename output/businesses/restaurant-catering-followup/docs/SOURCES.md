## Internal positioning note

The wedge should not be generic email marketing for restaurants. The sharper wedge is missed/repeat catering revenue: office managers, pharma reps, schools, nonprofits, real estate teams, clinics, and local companies who ordered once or asked for a quote but never received structured follow-up.
