# Frontend Views — Restaurant Catering Follow-Up Copilot

## Public marketing site

### 1. Home
- Hero: "Recover repeat catering orders without adding another manager."
- Subhero: for restaurants that receive catering calls, web forms, quotes, and one-time office orders but lack systematic follow-up.
- CTA: "Get a catering follow-up audit" / "Run a 30-day recovered revenue pilot".
- Problem section: quotes go cold, one-time customers are forgotten, staff follow-up lives in notebooks and inboxes.
- Money section: show simple calculator: average order value × missed follow-ups × close/reorder rate.
- Trust section: human-approved messages, no auto-discounts, no real customer data needed for demo.

### 2. Catering Revenue Calculator
Inputs:
- average catering order value,
- quote requests per month,
- one-time catering orders per month,
- estimated current follow-up rate,
- expected conversion/reorder lift.

Outputs:
- estimated monthly recoverable revenue,
- break-even pilot price,
- suggested follow-up volume.

### 3. Pilot Offer Page
- 30-day pilot for one location or one catering manager.
- Import last 90 days of catering quotes/orders via CSV or manual entry.
- Deliver: prioritized account list, follow-up scripts, weekly pipeline report, recovered revenue tally.
- CTA: paid pilot deposit.

### 4. Lead Capture
Fields: restaurant name, location count, catering URL/menu, average order value, monthly catering inquiries, POS/catering tools, contact info, consent.

## Authenticated restaurant app

### 1. Dashboard
- Today’s follow-ups due.
- Pipeline value by stage: new quote, hot quote, first-order follow-up, repeat account, lapsed.
- Recovered revenue this month.
- High-value accounts needing staff attention.
- Warnings: overdue follow-ups, missing phone/email, opt-outs.

### 2. Lead / Order Import
- CSV upload with mapping screen.
- Manual add lead/order.
- Paste from catering inquiry email.
- Validation preview: duplicates, missing fields, likely company/account match.

### 3. Account Detail
- Company/contact info.
- Order history and quote history.
- Dietary/menu notes.
- Follow-up timeline.
- Next recommended action.
- Draft messages and call notes.
- Staff notes and owner assignment.

### 4. Follow-Up Queue
- Filter by due today, overdue, high-value, quote pending, lapsed, seasonal.
- Each row: account, contact, event date, value estimate, stage, recommended message, assigned staff.
- Actions: approve/send externally, copy draft, mark called, snooze, disqualify, opt out.

### 5. Message Review
- Draft email/SMS/call note with personalization tokens highlighted.
- Menu and pricing caveats shown beside the draft.
- Required checks: event date, delivery address/radius, dietary/allergy notes, opt-out status.
- Staff can edit tone and CTA before export/send.

### 6. Campaigns
- Templates for: office lunch reorder, holiday party planning, school events, healthcare rep lunches, nonprofit board meetings, real estate open house trays.
- Calendar-based reminders with suggested send windows.

### 7. Reports
- Follow-ups completed.
- Quote-to-order conversion.
- Repeat order rate.
- Estimated recovered revenue.
- Top accounts by revenue and next event date.
- Export CSV for owner/operator.

### 8. Settings
- Restaurant brand voice.
- Catering menu links and minimums.
- Delivery radius/fee caveats.
- Opt-out rules.
- Staff users and roles.
- Integrations placeholders.
