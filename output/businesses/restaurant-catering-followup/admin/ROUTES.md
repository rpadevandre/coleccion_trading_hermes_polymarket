# Admin Routes

- `/admin/restaurant-catering-followup/cateringdashboard` — New inquiries, quotes due, booked revenue and lost value.
- `/admin/restaurant-catering-followup/leadboard` — Kanban by new/contacted/quoted/booked/lost.
- `/admin/restaurant-catering-followup/eventdetail` — Event requirements, quote checklist, notes and follow-up history.
- `/admin/restaurant-catering-followup/menupackagesettings` — Configure packages, menus, capacity and service areas.
- `/admin/restaurant-catering-followup/revenueanalytics` — Inquiry source, booking rate, average event value.
