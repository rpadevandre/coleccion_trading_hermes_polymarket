# Restaurant Catering Follow-Up Copilot

**Score:** 66/90
**Sector:** Restaurants / catering sales

## English-first positioning

Stop letting catering inquiries die in the inbox.

## Spanish adaptation

Deja de perder solicitudes de catering en el inbox.

## Target buyer

Restaurant owner/operator, catering manager, multi-location local restaurant group.

## Target user

Catering manager, front-of-house manager, owner.

## Problem

Restaurants miss catering revenue when inquiries from forms, calls, DMs and emails are not followed up quickly or consistently.

## Offer v0

AI-assisted catering lead follow-up: captures event details, drafts quotes/follow-ups, reminds staff and tracks booked catering revenue.
