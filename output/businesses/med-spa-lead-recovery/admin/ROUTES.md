# Admin Routes

- `/admin/med-spa-lead-recovery/leaddashboard` — New leads, hot consults, booked consults and recovered value.
- `/admin/med-spa-lead-recovery/leadqueue` — Prioritized follow-up queue by source and intent.
- `/admin/med-spa-lead-recovery/leaddetail` — Contact data, service interest, notes, drafts and history.
- `/admin/med-spa-lead-recovery/treatmentsettings` — Services, disclaimers, approved templates and routing rules.
- `/admin/med-spa-lead-recovery/revenueanalytics` — Lead source performance, booking rate and lost/recovered value.
