# Med Spa Lead Recovery

**Score:** 73/90
**Sector:** Med spa / aesthetics clinics

## English-first positioning

Turn missed med spa leads into booked consults.

## Spanish adaptation

Convierte leads perdidos de med spa en consultas agendadas.

## Target buyer

Med spa owner, clinic manager, aesthetics practice operator in US/Canada/UK/Australia.

## Target user

Front desk, sales coordinator, injector/aesthetician, owner.

## Problem

Med spas lose high-value consults when Instagram DMs, missed calls, form leads and SMS follow-ups are scattered or slow.

## Offer v0

AI-assisted lead recovery and consult booking workflow for med spas: captures inbound leads, qualifies intent, drafts follow-ups, books consults and reports lost/recovered revenue.
