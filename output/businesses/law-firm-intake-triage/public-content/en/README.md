# Law Firm Intake Triage — Public Content (EN)

## Positioning

Law Firm Intake Triage is an internal business concept for intake triage for small law firms.

## Audience

The first public story is aimed at small law firms, intake coordinators, and managing attorneys in English-speaking markets.

## Public narrative

The practical promise: sort inbound leads by practice area, urgency, jurisdiction fit, conflict risk, and next-step readiness without giving legal advice. The public content should stay focused on business outcomes, time saved, revenue recovered, risk reduced, or client retention improved.

## Content themes

1. The hidden cost of the current manual workflow.
2. Before/after examples using mock data only.
3. A narrow checklist or audit offer that validates willingness to pay.
4. Human-approved AI assistance rather than unsupervised automation.
5. Metrics a buyer can understand: saved hours, recovered revenue, faster response, cleaner handoff.

## Example post

> Most teams do not need another generic chatbot. They need a narrow workflow that turns messy inbound information into a clean next action.
>
> Law Firm Intake Triage focuses on one measurable operational bottleneck and packages it as an audit/pilot before a full product build.

## Lead magnet

A paid or low-cost audit using sanitized examples from the buyer. Deliver a concise report with gaps, suggested automation, templates, and ROI estimate.

## Disclaimer

Internal planning/public-content draft only. No production deployment, secrets, or real customer data are included in this repository.
