# Admin Routes

- `/admin/construction-bid-inbox/biddashboard` — Pipeline counts, due dates, potential value and bottlenecks.
- `/admin/construction-bid-inbox/bidqueue` — Prioritized bid inbox with fit score and deadline.
- `/admin/construction-bid-inbox/biddetail` — Scope summary, missing docs, risks and estimator notes.
- `/admin/construction-bid-inbox/nobidrules` — Configure bad-fit criteria and trade/geography limits.
- `/admin/construction-bid-inbox/estimatoranalytics` — Bid velocity, win/loss notes and opportunity value.
