# Business Plan — Construction Bid Inbox Copilot

## Goal

Build a small, monetizable AI workflow product/service for an English-speaking market with clear willingness to pay.

## Target buyer

Subcontractor owner, estimator, preconstruction manager, small GC.

## Pain

Estimators drown in bid invites, addenda, deadlines and proposal revisions. Missing one addendum can erase profit.

## Offer

AI bid inbox that extracts due dates, RFI deadlines, addenda, contacts, scope notes, and next actions from forwarded bid emails.

## Revenue model hypothesis

- Setup fee for done-for-you configuration.
- Monthly subscription/support.
- Optional paid pilot before full productization.

## Validation gate

Do not overbuild until there is at least one of:

- 2 discovery calls.
- 1 paid pilot/LOI.
- 5+ meaningful replies from target buyers.
- 1 operator willing to test fake/manual MVP flow.
