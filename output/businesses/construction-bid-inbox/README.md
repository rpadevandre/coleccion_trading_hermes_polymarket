# Construction Bid Inbox Copilot

**Score:** 69/90

## English-first positioning

Your bid inbox should not be your preconstruction system.

## Spanish adaptation

Tu inbox de licitaciones no debería ser tu sistema de preconstrucción.

## Target buyer

Subcontractor owner, estimator, preconstruction manager, small GC.

## Target user

Estimator handling bid invites, addenda, RFIs, quote leveling and proposal emails.

## Problem

Estimators drown in bid invites, addenda, deadlines and proposal revisions. Missing one addendum can erase profit.

## Offer v0

AI bid inbox that extracts due dates, RFI deadlines, addenda, contacts, scope notes, and next actions from forwarded bid emails.
