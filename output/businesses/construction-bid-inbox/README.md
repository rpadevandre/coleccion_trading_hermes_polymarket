# Construction Bid Inbox Copilot

**Score:** 78/90

## English-first positioning

Your bid inbox should not be your preconstruction system.

## Spanish adaptation

Tu inbox de licitaciones no debería ser tu sistema de preconstrucción.

## Target buyer

English-speaking subcontractor owners, estimating managers, preconstruction managers and small GCs in the US/Canada/UK/AUS who receive bid invites by email but do not want a heavy enterprise preconstruction rollout.

## Target user

Estimator handling invite-to-bid emails, addenda, RFI deadlines, quote leveling, bid/no-bid notes and proposal follow-ups.

## Problem

Estimators drown in bid invites, addenda, deadlines and proposal revisions. Missing one addendum or bid deadline can erase profit, damage GC relationships or waste estimating hours on poor-fit work.

## Offer v0

A lightweight AI bid inbox copilot that watches a forwarded mailbox, extracts due dates/RFI deadlines/addenda/contact/scope signals, suggests bid/no-bid next steps, and gives the owner a clean weekly pipeline view.

## Money-focused validation wedge

Start as a concierge workflow for 3-5 subcontractors at **$500 setup + $300-$750/mo**. The paid promise is not “AI”; it is fewer missed deadlines, faster triage and better focus on profitable bids.
