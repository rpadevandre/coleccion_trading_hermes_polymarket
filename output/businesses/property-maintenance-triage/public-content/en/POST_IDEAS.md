# EN Build-in-Public Ideas — Property Maintenance Triage

1. Building a workflow for operators who still run critical work through voicemail, SMS, inboxes and spreadsheets.
2. The problem: Tenants send vague maintenance texts at all hours; managers waste time classifying urgency, requesting photos, routing vendors and documenting response.
3. The first MVP is not full automation. It is cleaner intake, routing and decision support.
