# Admin Routes

- `/admin/property-maintenance-triage/portfoliodashboard` — Open issues, emergencies, aging tickets and vendor workload.
- `/admin/property-maintenance-triage/requestqueue` — Triage queue with category, urgency and missing info.
- `/admin/property-maintenance-triage/requestdetail` — Tenant message, extracted facts, suggested route and audit log.
- `/admin/property-maintenance-triage/vendorsettings` — Vendors by trade, coverage, availability and contact rules.
- `/admin/property-maintenance-triage/slaanalytics` — Resolution time, emergency rate and repeated-property issues.
