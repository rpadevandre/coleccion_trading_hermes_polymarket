# Business Validation Ranking — First Cold Outreach Focus

Purpose: decide what to validate first when Andre returns to the PC. This ranking is based on speed to validate, clarity of pain, B2B willingness to pay, lead availability, and MVP simplicity.

Scoring scale: 1-5 per criterion.

Criteria:

- Economic pain: direct money/time loss.
- Buyer clarity: easy to identify decision maker.
- Lead availability: public contacts are findable.
- MVP simplicity: can demonstrate value with a lightweight prototype/manual workflow.
- Compliance risk: lower score means more risk; higher score means easier to operate safely.
- Speed to feedback: can get meaningful reply/interview quickly.

---

## Recommended ranking

## 1. Med Spa Lead Recovery

Score: 26/30

Why it ranks high:

- Clear revenue pain: unbooked consults and missed follow-ups.
- Public emails are relatively easy to find.
- Buyer is usually owner/operator/manager.
- Strong English-speaking market in USA.
- MVP can start as a lead-leak checklist + dashboard mockup.

Risks:

- Must avoid medical claims.
- Must avoid handling sensitive patient/health information in early MVP.

Best first CTA:

```text
Want me to send a 1-page lead recovery dashboard mockup?
```

Recommended first batch:

```text
5-10 med spas with public contact emails
```
