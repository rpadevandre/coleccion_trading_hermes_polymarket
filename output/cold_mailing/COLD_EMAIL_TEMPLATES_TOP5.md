## 1. HVAC Missed-Call Recovery

### Target
Small/medium HVAC companies with emergency service, maintenance plans, or high call volume.

### Hypothesis
HVAC owners lose high-value jobs because missed calls, after-hours requests, and web forms are not recovered fast enough.

### Subject options
- Quick question about missed HVAC calls
- Are after-hours HVAC leads slipping through?
- Idea for recovering missed HVAC jobs

### Email 1 — short
```text
Hi {{first_name}},

I noticed {{company}} handles HVAC service calls, and I’m testing a small tool for HVAC teams that helps recover missed calls and after-hours leads before they turn into lost jobs.

The idea is simple: capture missed calls/forms, classify urgency, and give the owner or dispatcher a daily recovery list with the highest-value leads first.

Would it be useful if I sent a 1-page example of what that recovery dashboard would look like for an HVAC company?

Best,
{{sender_name}}
```

### Follow-up 1
```text
Hi {{first_name}},

Quick follow-up — the main use case is not replacing dispatch software. It is catching the revenue leaks around it:

- missed calls
- after-hours requests
- no-response web forms
- emergency jobs that should be prioritized

Would a simple missed-lead audit be useful for {{company}}?
```

### Follow-up 2
```text
Hi {{first_name}},

Should I close the loop here?

I’m looking for 3-5 HVAC companies to validate whether missed-call recovery is worth turning into a small monthly tool.

If this is not relevant, no worries — just reply "not interested" and I won’t follow up.
```

### CTA variants
- Want me to send the 1-page dashboard example?
- Would a free missed-lead audit be useful?
- Open to a 10-minute call to see if this fits?

# Campaign Notes

## First validation campaign

Recommended first send:

```text
5 emails per business × 5 businesses = 25 emails total
```

Do not scale until we log:

- reply rate
- positive replies
- objections
- bounces
- opt-outs
- segment notes

## What to learn

For each reply, classify:

- problem exists / does not exist
- currently solved manually / with software / ignored
- willingness to review mockup
- willingness to pay
- best buyer title
- best CTA

## Do not send yet

Before sending real emails, confirm:

- sender domain/email
- unsubscribe/opt-out wording
- target geography
- exact offer
- whether a landing/mockup exists for the CTA
